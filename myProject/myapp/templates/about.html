ABOUT ROSHANNE

Excellence in modeling representation since 2019

OUR STORY

Founded in 2019, Roshanne Modeling Agency has been a beacon of excellence in the fashion industry for over six years.

What started as a small boutique agency with a vision for discovering unique talent has grown into one of the most respected modeling agencies in the industry. Our founder, Roshan Urbano, began with a simple philosophy: true beauty comes from authenticity, confidence, and the courage to be different.

Over the years, we have represented hundreds of models who have graced the covers of international magazines, walked the runways of Paris, Milan, and New York, and starred in major advertising campaigns for luxury brands worldwide.

Our success is built on three core principles: discovering exceptional talent, nurturing their growth, and creating opportunities that transform careers. We believe that every model we represent is not just a face, but a storyteller who brings brands to life.
